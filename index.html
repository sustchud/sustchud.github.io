Bio

Dr. Daning Hu is an Associate Professor (with Tenure) at the Business School of Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Shenzhen, China.

Prior to joining SUSTech, he was an Assistant Professor at the University of Zurich. He has taught at the University of Arizona, Zhejiang University, and City University of Hong Kong.

His research focuses on FinTech, network analytics, and the intersection of econometrics and machine learning. His work has appeared in MIS Quarterly, JAIS, Decision Support Systems, JASIST, and IEEE/ACM Transactions.
