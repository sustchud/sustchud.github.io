POLICY FOR RECOMMENDATION LETTERS


Thank you very much for considering my recommendation might be helpful and asked me for the letter. However, I cannot write a letter with sufficiently useful information for those who only took my courses or only work with me briefly. Currently I adopt the following policy for writing recommendation letters.

  1. For a letter to support one's PhD or Master applications, the student should work closely with me for at least 12 months.
  2. For a letter to support one's application of an award such as (e.g., National Scholarships), the student should work closely with me for at least 6 months.
  3. The above requirements are mandatory and basic, only those who have very solid achievements (publications, algorithms, tools or systems) will be considered for my recommendations.
